Horizon Diagram

Drag the diagram to rotate your view of the sky, or drag the sun disk to change the time of day or day of year (selected in General Settings). With the keyboard, click (or Tab to) the diagram and use the arrow keys to rotate the view; click the sun first and the arrow keys move the sun instead (Page Up / Page Down for larger steps). The Time of Day and Day of Year controls do the same.
